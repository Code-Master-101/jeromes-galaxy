You may ask, "What was the reason why he created this blog?" Well, I did it because of three reasons, first, because it was a school project (specifically my English project), second, I felt that I wanted to show my HTML and CSS prowess to others, and third, I wanted to share my personal life, both past and present (with some boundaries). I needed and wanted to do this blog.

So I promise, from today to when I decide to shut down this website (which will be a long time), I will update this website with a great story, every day or every other day. Have a nice day, and if you see a new blog, check it out! You might find a interesting story.
