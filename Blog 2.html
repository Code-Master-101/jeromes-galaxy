Blog II

The COVID-19 (coronavirus) pandemic - one of the deadliest pandemics in this century, with over 126 million cases, and 2.76 million deaths worldwide. It has separated millions of families, has left millions in the hospital fighting for their lives, and left people dead. So today, this blog will share how it affected the Earth and I.

On March 15, 2020, the Philippines was officially under a lockdown. Over the following days, more and more countries were lockdown, with India having the largest lockdown. However, due to violations of guidelines, as well as slow response from international governments, more and more became infected, and and some even died. I just hope that people become more aware of the requirements of sanitation, masks, and other COVID-19 guidelines.

It also affected my personal life. No longer did I have the freedom to go outside and meet up with friends. I couldn't go and visit my relatives from afar. My family and I stayed at home, praying that we, along with others, may be spared from the virus. Face-to-face classes weren't allowed anymore, which I am still missing to this day. In short, the pandemic changed everything about my life and force me to adapt. Have a nice day, and as always, see you next time (and please, wear a mask when you go out).
